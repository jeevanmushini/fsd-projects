About HackTheFuture 2023

HackTheFuture is IIT Bombay's annual flagship hackathon that brings together the brightest minds from across the country to solve real-world problems through technology. Now in its 8th edition, this year's theme is "Sustainable Futures Through Technology".

Event Highlights

  • 36-hour hackathon with mentorship from industry experts
  • Prizes worth ₹5 lakhs across multiple categories
  • Workshops on cutting-edge technologies
  • Networking opportunities with tech leaders
  • Campus accommodation and meals provided

Who Can Participate?

This event is open to all undergraduate and postgraduate students from any recognized university or college. Teams of 2-4 members can participate. Solo participants are also welcome but will be grouped with others during the event.

Frequently Asked Questions

The hackathon is open to all undergraduate and postgraduate students from any recognized university or college in India. International students are also welcome but must arrange their own travel and accommodation beyond what we provide.

Teams should consist of 2-4 members. Solo participants are allowed to register but will be grouped with other solo participants during the team formation session on Day 1.

We provide basic accommodation in campus hostels for all registered participants. Meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner) and snacks will be provided throughout the event. Please mention any dietary restrictions during registration.

Bring your laptop, charger, any specialty hardware you plan to use, valid college ID, and personal toiletries. We'll provide extension cords, WiFi, and basic hardware components.

Projects are judged on 4 criteria: Innovation (30%), Technical Complexity (30%), Potential Impact (20%), and Presentation (20%). Each team will have 5 minutes to demo their project to judges.
