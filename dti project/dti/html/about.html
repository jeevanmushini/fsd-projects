About TechEvents Hub

Connecting students with the best technical events across India's top institutions

Our Mission

TechEvents Hub was founded in 2023 with a simple goal: to make it easier for students to discover and participate in technical events across India's colleges and universities.

We believe that hackathons, coding competitions, and tech fests are invaluable opportunities for students to learn, network, and showcase their skills. However, finding information about these events can be challenging, especially for students from smaller institutions.

Our Story

2021

The Idea Was Born

Our founder, a college student at the time, struggled to find information about hackathons outside his own university. He realized there was no centralized platform for technical events across institutions.

2022

First Prototype

Working with a small team of fellow students, the first version of TechEvents Hub was created as a simple directory of upcoming events.

Early 2023

Official Launch

After positive feedback from beta testers, we officially launched TechEvents Hub with events from 50+ top technical institutions.

Today

Growing Community

We've grown to become India's most comprehensive platform for student tech events, with thousands of active users every month.
